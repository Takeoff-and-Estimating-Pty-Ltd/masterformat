## ğŸ“‹ What is MasterFormat?

MasterFormat is the construction industry's standard for organizing specifications and project information in the United States and Canada. Often called the **"Dewey Decimal System" of construction**, it provides a consistent framework for:

- Writing and organizing specifications
- Cost estimating and budgeting
- Bid management
- Project management
- Procurement

Developed by the **Construction Specifications Institute (CSI)** and **Construction Specifications Canada (CSC)**.
